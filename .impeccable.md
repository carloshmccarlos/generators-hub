## Design Context

### Users
Content creators, social media managers, data analysts, and developers who need quick generators for TikTok comments and mathematical sequences. Used in office/work contexts during daytime hours, primarily on desktops but increasingly on tablets.

### Brand Personality
Editorial, refined, breathable — like a high-end magazine (Vogue/Kinfolk) meets developer tool. The interface should feel premium, light, and airy while maintaining technical precision.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Theme**: Light mode (daytime professional use)
- **Tone**: Editorial elegance with technical precision
- **References**: Apple's clean UI, Kinfolk magazine layouts, modern fashion editorial typography
- **Anti-references**: Neon/glow effects, heavy shadows, dark mode, generic AI gradients
- **Visual approach**: Creamy warm backgrounds (#faf9f5), sleek black accents, thin borders, generous whitespace, frosted glass effects

### Design Principles
1. **Breathability**: Give elements room to breathe — generous padding, short line lengths, spacious layouts
2. **Editorial Typography**: Bold geometric display fonts for headings, refined sans for body, uppercase mono for labels
3. **Subtle Elevation**: Use soft shadows and frosted glass, never harsh neon or heavy effects
4. **High Contrast**: Sleek black accents on warm cream backgrounds for clarity and elegance
5. **Touch-Friendly**: Large touch targets, generous spacing for accessibility
